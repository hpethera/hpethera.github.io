Trisha scriptor iuvante sequi
Sleep children,
Sleep soundly together, in your warm,
comfy and good bed.
 
I watch over you, always you,
I will always be by your side, even alone, don't worry,
I am here by your side.
 
In the realm of sleep,
In a field of flowers, sleep children,
Sleep soundly with me.
 
Don't worry, children,
Even if father is not here, don't worry, children,
Your mother is here.
 
I watch over you, always you,
I will always be by your side, children, without feeling,
Go quickly to sleep.
 
In their bedroom, without mother.
In their bedroom, till the day arrives.
